Characteristics

Iroha asobe contains a lot of characteristics and functions, among which stand out.

Utility

She has a wide variety of useful commands. In which they are included search and informative commands, thanks to those commands you will not need to leave discord to search anything!.

Moderation

You can moderate members by kicking, muting, warning, ect. She has a wide variety of useful commands that will make your discord experience much better!

Anime

With her anime commands you can search anime characters, fan arts and get random waifus!

Fun

She also has fun commands! Also you can make memes or get the most popular memes!

Interaction and reaction

Interact and react with your friends using her commands!

Configuration

You will be able to set or change parameters with Iroha Asobe, for example: Set the Welcome / Set the suggestions channel, a custom Prefix and much more!
